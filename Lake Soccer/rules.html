Lake Ukickee Soccer

League Rules

General Conduct

All players, coaches, and spectators are expected to maintain sportsmanship and respect throughout the league. Unsportsmanlike behavior will not be tolerated and may result in penalties or ejection from the league.

Match Duration

Each match will consist of two halves, each lasting 40 minutes, with a 10-minute halftime break. The referee has final authority on all timekeeping decisions.

Team Composition

Each team must field a minimum of 7 players to start a match and may have up to 18 players on their roster. Substitutions can be made on the fly, but the total number of players on the field cannot exceed 11 at any time.

Equipment

  • Players must wear appropriate soccer cleats (no metal studs).
  • Shin guards are mandatory for all players during matches.
  • Team jerseys should be clearly numbered and distinguishable from opponents.

Fouls and Penalties

Standard FIFA soccer rules apply for fouls, free kicks, penalty kicks, and cards. Referees have full discretion on penalties and issuing cautions or dismissals.

Field and Goals

Matches are played on regulation-size fields with full-sized goals. The dimensions and markings conform to US Youth Soccer guidelines.
