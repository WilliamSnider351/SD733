Welcome to Lake Ukickee Soccer

Lake Ukickee Soccer is proud to be part of a premier Women’s Spring Training Camp League, dedicated to developing elite players for the professional, collegiate, and Olympic levels.

Our program is open to athletes aged 18 and up who are serious about advancing their soccer careers.

We offer:

  • World-class coaching
  • Technical skill development
  • Strength & conditioning training
  • Endurance running and fitness programs
  • Full housing accommodations and nutritious meals
  • Official team uniforms
  • Spring matches between league teams

Lake Ukickee Soccer builds athletes who are prepared for the next level — whether that’s college recruitment, pro trials, or Olympic development. Come train like a champion in the Finger Lakes.
